# Privacy Policy for Tab Guard

**Effective Date:** December 17, 2025

## Overview

Tab Guard is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

## Data Collection

Tab Guard does **not** collect any user data. Specifically:

- No personally identifiable information
- No browsing history
- No website content
- No user activity tracking
- No analytics or telemetry

## Data Storage

The only data stored by Tab Guard is your tab limit preference, which is:

- Stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
- Never transmitted to external servers
- Never shared with third parties
- Accessible only by the extension itself

## Permissions

Tab Guard requests the following permissions:

- **tabs**: To count open tabs and close tabs when the limit is reached
- **windows**: To count open windows and display this information
- **storage**: To save your tab limit preference locally
- **notifications**: To notify you when you've reached your tab limit

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.

## Third-Party Services

Tab Guard does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in future versions of the extension and posted at this URL.
